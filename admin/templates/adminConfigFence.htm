Fence List Variables
       
  Fence listing is similar to Black/White listing, it allows you to filter traffic to specific systems or networks. However, Fence listing does not filter inbound connections to the honeypots. Instead, Fence listing specifies what systems the honeypots cannot talk to or initiate a connection to. This is a security prevention measure, usually used to protect critical systems.
